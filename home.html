    Project Description

    During this intersession, students, led by Ms. Alper and Mr. Carr, learned about optics, how light works and bends, and astronomy, the study of our galaxy. Throughout the course of three weeks, students made their own lenses and telescopes, and learned how light refracted. In addition, students stargazed and learned about celestial coordinate systems and how to study the cosmos. For their final project, students worked together to create a model solar system. They used celestial coordinates to map where each planet was on September 4, 2019, at 8:30am, the first day the highschool was open. We hope you enjoy!
